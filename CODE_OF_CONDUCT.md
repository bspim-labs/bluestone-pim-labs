# Code of Conduct

Bluestone PIM Labs is a community space for builders. Keep it useful and respectful.

- Be direct and constructive. Criticism of projects and ideas is fine. Personal attacks are not.
- Stay on topic. This is a space for Bluestone PIM builders, not general debate.
- No spam, self-promotion without substance, or misleading project listings.

If something feels off, open an issue or reach out directly. This community is small enough that most things can be resolved with a conversation.
